Sex On The Beach

This Sex on the Beach recipe makes a refreshing cocktail of vodka, peach schnapps, cranberry, and orange juice. It can be served over ice as a drink or strained into a shot or martini glass.


Ingredients:

  • 1 Fluid Ounce Vodka
  • 1 Fluid Ounce Peach Schnapps
  • 3 Fluid Ounces Orange Juice
  • 3 FLuid Ounces Cranberry Juice

Directions:

  1. Step 1: Fill a highball glass with ice, pour in vodka and peach schnapps. Add in orange juice and cranberry juice.
